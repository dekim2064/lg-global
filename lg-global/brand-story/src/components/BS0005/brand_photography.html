Photography

We have three different types of photography, each with their own clear role to play.
All of our photography is unified by clear principles: authenticity, positivity, warmth,
breathing space, diversity and attention to detail.

  • 01Everyday Life

  • 02Energy, Passion and Interests

  • 03Products

Everyday Life

We capture real, relatable and spontaneous moments.
Our imagery should feel warm, positive and relatable.

Energy, Passion
and Interests

We capture and celebrate the energy and attitude of people and their passions
- whether that be gaming, fitness, fashion or food. Our imagery should feel bold,
positive, vibrant and in motion.

Products

We capture the craft, detail and the quality of our products. Always balancing warmth and
innovation in a way that feels uniquely LG.
