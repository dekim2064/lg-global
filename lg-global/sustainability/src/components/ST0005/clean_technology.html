Upgrade your experience
UP home appliances

UP home appliances surpass the conventional use and management of products by incorporating new functionalities. These enhancements are continuously upgraded in consideration of valuable input from consumers. LG Electronics has introduced UP home appliances, featuring 24 different products such as "Laundry Care after End" and "Pet Care," along with over 150 upgrade features.

Among these, eco-conscious UP appliances offer an energy-saving cycle for dishwashers and a microplastic care program for washing machines. The energy-saving cycle maintains the same cleaning power as the standard cycle but consumes 20% less electricity. Additionally, the washing machine's microplastic care program reduces cloth friction, resulting in a 70% reduction in the release of microplastics caused by synthetic fiber damage.

Towards zero ozone layer depletion
Heat pump technology

Heat pump technology utilizes the eco-friendly refrigerant "R290" to achieve a remarkable milestone in ozone depletion potential (ODP) with a rating of 0.

This innovative air conditioning system replaces traditional heaters and enables cooling and heating using air, land, and water as heat sources. By doing so, it saves up to 80% energy compared to conventional air conditioning systems.

Empowering the future for environmental protection
Glass powder

LG Electronics continuously develops functional materials as key drivers of future growth. This includes the production of antibacterial glass powder and water-soluble glass powder that completely dissolves in water. Glass powder is composed of fine particles obtained by crushing glass and possesses excellent material stability and durability. In 2013, LG Electronics introduced an oven in North America featuring the "Easy Clean" function, which utilizes a coating of functional glass powder on the metal surface, making it effortless to clean the inside.

The versatile antibacterial glass powder is applicable to many appliances, especially those with handles that contact with the body, such as refrigerators, washing machines, and air conditioners. Also, water-soluble glass powder transforms into inorganic ions when dissolved in water, facilitating the growth of microalgae and seaweed in the ocean. It can also serve as an alternative to ocher spray used during red tide occurrences.

Recreation of product
New cycle-crafted products
We've made our products more eco-conscious than ever, by using recycled plastic, recycled PET bottles for fabric covering, and molded pulp made with recycled paper and cardboard for packaging. Our effort helps us reduce plastic waste, and lower our carbon footprint and CO2 emissions.

LG OLED
EVO

LG OLED Evo utilizes composite fiber materials and metal instead of plastic, resulting in reduced weight and increased durability. It decreases plastic usage by 40% compared to LCD TVs, leading to a reduction of 20,000 tons annually. The product has been recognized by certifications such as the Carbon Footprint Certification from the UK's Carbon Trust, eco-friendly certification from Switzerland's SGS, and ECV Environmental Claim Validation by UL in the US. The manufacturing process also emits less carbon dioxide and minimizes damage risks.

LG Sound Bar

Our 2022 LG sound bar lineup is both SGS Eco-Product and ENERGY STAR-certified, with parts made from recycled plastic resin and polyester jersey fabric, meeting the Global Recycled Standard.

LG tiiun
mini

tiiun mini is made from UP (Upcycled Plastic), replacing the conventional plastic used in the previous model (L011W1) with PCR (Post-Consumer Recycled) plastic.

LG Aero
Furniture

LG Aero Furniture is made by blending PCR (Post Consumer Recycled) plastics, recycled from consumer-used plastic waste, with mineral particles to form a distinctive 'TerraZo texture.'

Our commitment to sustainability was recognized with the Product Champion Award by the US Environmental Protection Agency, acknowledging our dedication to delivering sustainable innovations.

Making better use of available
resources

LG is working to improve its products' performance and convenience while minimizing volume and weight through the development of materials and structures. We reduced the weight of one of our refrigerator models (model: K410MC19E) by 2% in 2020 by changing materials and increased the use of recycled materials by 6% for ten types of plastic.
We have expanded the use of recycled materials in various products, including washing machines, refrigerators, air conditioners, smartphones, TVs, and monitors, with over 11,000 tons of recycled plastic used in 2020.

LG tiiun exemplifies our dedication to exemplary ESG management. Each unit incorporates components crafted from recycled plastics, effectively reducing carbon emissions throughout the production process.
We promote the development of alternative parts and technologies for all products, and carefully assess the performance of recycled plastics we use.

New Uses of Packaging
Materials

We're committed to minimizing our negative impact on the environment, from design to delivery. We use foam plastic instead of Styrofoam packaging for air conditioning systems and outdoor units, and we've reduced the amount of paper used to pack an outdoor unit from 2,950g to 300g.

Outdoor unit packaging is also collected for washing and recycling, which we expect will reduce around 85 tons of paper and 19 tons of Styrofoam annually. Through this, we're actively promoting sustainability and reducing waste.
