Reduction of greenhouse gas emissions during the production stage (~2030)
Our goal is to decrease greenhouse gas emissions during production by 50% compared to 2017 using advanced equipment and devices. Additionally, we'll actively engage in Clean Development Mechanism (CDM*) projects to acquire carbon credits and support developing nations in reducing their emissions. We'll also implement our own solutions, like upgraded refrigerators and Energy Management Systems (EMS), which promote the use of renewable energy.
*CDM: Clean Development Mechanism
Switching to 100% renewable energy (~2050)
We established a long-term plan to achieve 100% renewable energy use and expand the transition to renewable energy, taking into account the current laws and status of business sites in Korea and other countries where we operate. Readily available options include green tariffs for purchasing renewable energy, Renewable Energy Credits (REC) purchases, and third-party Power Purchase Agreements (PPA).
Securing Certified Emissions Reductions
LG aims to reduce carbon emissions and stay carbon neutral by developing high-efficiency products and investing in greenhouse gas reduction projects. We received a carbon emissions allowance for providing India with high-efficiency fridges equipped with linear inverter compressors that improve energy efficiency and performance. Profits from this initiative are re-invested in greenhouse gas reduction projects in Korea, creating a virtuous cycle of eco-conscious action.

Carbon emission reduction

First US
Lighthouse Factory
LG Tennessee Plant

LG Electronics' factory in Tennessee, USA was
chosen by the World Economic Forum as a
"lighthouse factory" for its advanced technology and
automation. This factory produces home appliances
like washing machines and dryers, and is the first US-
based consumer electronics factory to earn this
prestigious designation.

The factory incorporates cutting-edge digital technologies such as AI,
big data, IoT, and robots. With a capacity to produce 1.2 million
washers and 600,000 dryers annually, it efficiently meets the evolving
market demand through local production. The facility takes pride in its
fully automated logistics system, which features 166 automated
guided vehicles (AGVs) and utilizes robots for complex tasks like
assembling and handling heavy components. The factory's
sustainability commitment is evident through its complete transition
to renewable energy and the use of a high-efficiency utility
infrastructure.

First Home Appliances
Lighthouse Factory
LG Smart Park

Changwon, Korea

In March 2022, Changwon LG Smart Park was named
the first 'lighthouse factory' in the Korean home
appliance industry. It's been acknowledged worldwide
for its innovative, energy-efficient production line.

LG Smart Park is all about eco-conscious manufacturing. We cut
greenhouse gases, use eco-conscious production technology and
advanced energy facilities, recycle waste heat, and use BECON, the
latest energy management solution, to reduce energy consumption by
30%. Plus, we build solar facilities to boost renewable energy
production. This will eliminate more than 10% of our electricity use,
reducing as much greenhouse gas emissions as planting 20,000 trees
annually. It means we're doing our part in creating a sustainable
future.

B2B activity

What is
Net Zero House?

Net Zero houses are built with sustainability
in mind, producing and storing renewable
energy, using energy-efficient appliances and
ACs, and limiting environmental impact with
eco-conscious materials. You can also monitor
your energy use and lead a sustainable lifestyle
through ThinQ technology.

Energy package by space
: Garage

The energy package for the garage is a
sustainable energy system that generates
energy with solar panels, stores energy in an
Energy Storage System (ESS), and then
supplies it to power household appliances.

Energy storage system (ESS)

An Energy Storage System (ESS) stores
electricity generated from renewable
energy sources and efficiently dispenses
it at optimum times to help reduce
electricity costs.

Energy package by space
: Bath & Room

The energy package for bath and room features
high-efficiency system boilers that use air heat
instead of gas for home and water heating, as
well as premium ventilation that recovers
energy by reducing heat loss, while preserving
high air quality.

Eco system boiler

Absorbs heat in the air to produce hot
water that can also be used for home
heating. Reduced energy consumption
lowers carbon emissions.

Premium ventilation system

Premium window ventilation lets out
polluted air, while also generating
heating or cooling energy. By expelling
only polluted air 60% of cooling energy
and 74% of heating energy is recovered,
resulting in significant energy savings.

Energy package by space
: City / Commercial Facility

Explore LG's vision for a smart city in this
space. Energy-efficient AC systems can
efficiently store and utilize power as needed
for large-scale facilities, battery materials,
products, and other related services.

AI temperature system

The AI temperature system optimizes
air conditioning and saves energy by
learning about the usage environment.
It predicts power consumption before
use for energy-saving customized
control, and adjusts to the most
efficient setting based on automatic
external weather updates.
