Climate Action

Big steps.
Little carbon.

Nature always had our attention. But its message is urgent:
to reduce carbon emissions. As part of the cause, we're now a
bigger part of the solution. LG Electronics is committed to an
aggressive schedule toward carbon-neutrality and using 100%
renewable energy. The sooner we minimize our impact, there
will be fewer "natural" hazards, and more nature to appreciate.

Circular Economy

Sustainable Cycle

Imagine a world with no waste. That's what
we strive for with our sustainable cycle. By
recycling all we can to turn old materials and
appliances into new products, we're doing
our part to reduce waste and create a more
sustainable future for our planet.
