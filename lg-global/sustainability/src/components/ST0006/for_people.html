Unable to press a button? Simply say “Hi, LG.”
Voice-activated water purifier
The LG PuriCare Objet Collection redefines water purifiers by introducing voice recognition. With this technology, users only have to say “Hi LG, pour 250ml of hot water”. For example, let’s say you cannot use your hands because you are cooking. Simply tell the water purifier how much water you want and how warm or cold you want it, and it will start working all by itself.
The user-friendly design includes voice guidance, benefiting individuals with visual impairments, the elderly, and children who may find buttons challenging. For example, when visually impaired individuals use a water purifier to drink tea, the voice guidance will tell them, "Be careful, hot water is coming out," to use the product more safely. This innovative solution ensures convenient use for everyone.

Voice recognition

The product knows each user's voice and starts operating when the user says a command without touching the product.

Opening doors is no longer a manual task
Adding convenience to refrigerator
Discover the convenience and intelligent design of the LG Signature Refrigerator, thoughtfully created to cater to the unique needs of different individuals. For instance, imagine you are holding lots of ingredients or your hands are messy from cooking. The refrigerator's door opens like magic, so you can effortlessly reach for whatever you need inside. This feature is especially helpful for people who use wheelchairs or have trouble with their hands.
Introducing functions of refrigerator
Auto lift

With a simple press of a button, the bottom drawer automatically opens, while the containers inside elevate to a comfortable knee height. This thoughtful design ensures easy access and usability for individuals using wheelchairs or those with limited dexterity in their hands.

Easy handle

The product door can be effortlessly opened with minimal force, and the handle, as well as the direction of door opening, can be adjusted to the left or right, enhancing convenience.

Auto open door

When you stand in the front of refrigerator, the sensor located at the bottom of the right refrigerator door detects your foot and opens the door automatically.

* Please note that this feature is applicable only to the right door of the refrigerator compartment.

Universal usability for all
LG OLED TV
We understand that using a TV can be challenging for seniors, people with disabilities, and those who have trouble with devices. For instance, when living with seniors or people with disabilities, it can be difficult to find the right TV volume, subtitle location, or sign language size that suits everyone's needs. To help with this, LG Electronics actively involves a diverse range of customers in the planning of our products and services. Our aim is to create a TV that is accessible and user-friendly for everyone.
Introducing functions of TV
Read the menu aloud

The TV provides voice guidance for menu names and operations, making it more convenient for visually impaired users. After enabling the function, you can adjust the voice speed, volume, and pitch level according to your preferences.

Voice commentary on the screen

For visually impaired users, the TV offers voice explanations of the content displayed on the screen.

* This feature is only available when the TV program supports it.

Listen to the TV together

We have incorporated a feature that allows simultaneous sound output to both the TV speaker and a Bluetooth device. This enables customers with hearing loss caused by disabilities or aging to enjoy watching TV with their family or friends. However, for Bluetooth devices, we recommend using earphones or headphones.

* This feature is only available when the TV program supports it.

Subtitles

Having multiple subtitles can be overwhelming. Our devices allow you to adjust the position of the subtitles to your preferred location on the screen using the direction buttons on the remote control. By doing so, you can ensure that the subtitles do not overlap, making them easier to read and understand.

* Note that this feature is only available when the broadcasting program supports subtitles.

Enlarging sign language

The feature makes the sign language screen bigger on TV. It helps people who have trouble hearing and use sign language to understand better. People who have difficulties hearing said that when the sign language screen is bigger, they can see the mouth and hand shapes more clearly. It is like turning up the volume on the TV for someone who can hear.

* Note that this feature is only available when the broadcasting program supports sign language.

Services designed for everyone
Visual support
Braille stickers
Easy "Touch" feature for those with difficulties

We understand the challenges faced by visually impaired individuals when using touch-operated home appliances. To address this problem, we have developed Braille stickers with raised dots. These stickers enable people with visual impairments to easily identify and access functions of their appliances. They can also be used on other devices. We are proud to provide these stickers free of charge, ensuring accessibility for all.

* Please note that Braille stickers are only provided in Korea

Elderly support
Senior care program
To make it easy for seniors to use

We recognize the importance of usability for seniors and understands that even the best products can be challenging for them. Seniors often struggle with small font sizes and find it difficult to comprehend manuals. To better support our senior users, we offer various services beyond traditional approaches. This includes a dedicated counseling service specifically tailored for seniors and the "visible ARS" and "slow talk ARS" options. These options display information in large letters, catering to customers aged 60 and above who are registered at the customer center. For customers aged 70 and above, a specialized team is directly accessible. Additionally, we introduced the "Senior Customer Family Feedback Program.“ This program ensures that families or caretakers of senior customers receive reassuring texts and detailed updates on complaint resolutions, keeping them informed throughout the process.

* Please note that this program is only provided in Korea

Hearing support
Sign language counseling service
Can be done by hand

Sign language is a unique language. Hearing impaired individuals who are more comfortable with sign language may find it challenging to understand written Korean (Hangul), even if they know the language. This is why we provide a service where individuals can seek help in sign language for complex processes.
At LG Electronics, professional sign language interpreters offer sign language interpretation services for conversations between service engineers and customers. This service, which launched in 2021, is part of our commitment to ensuring that all consumers using our products can easily access and utilize them without any inconvenience or difficulty. The sign language counseling center is gradually expanding its coverage of consultation, services, and rentals.

* Please note that this service is only provided in Korea

Shin Jeong-eun , Managing director

“My goal is to become a female leader who
cultivates an organizational culture of
diversity and inclusivity, providing members
with confidence to develop their skills and
fostering a genuine sense of belonging.”

What are you currently in charge of?

My team uses data to create smart home technology tailored to customers' lifestyles. By studying their daily lives and combining data across industries, we unlock the value of converged data with advanced AI analysis.

When did you join LG Electronics and what was your first position?

In May 2010, I joined LG Electronics CTO Division, focusing on the multimedia lab. Initially, I worked on camera image processing for vehicle safety, developing innovative solutions using front and rear cameras to recognize and protect pedestrians and surroundings when the vehicle is parked. LG Electronics actively supports advanced technology development, especially for smart car parts, with the Vehicle Component Solution (VS) Division driving future growth.

As a female executive, what kind of organization do you feel LG Electronics is?

Although the proportion of female executives has increased recently, it is still a minority, and it is not easy to stand out even more in large companies such as LG Electronics. Here, I even feel the weight of the title of the youngest female executive born in the 80s. But rather, as a young executive, I think I can work freely and comfortably with MZ generation employees. The open environment and culture of LG Electronics, which provides more opportunities and support to young and competent juniors, is also very good.
Especially this year, it was very meaningful to me that a female team leader born in the 1990s was appointed as a team leader through a lot of mentoring in our lab. People around me are giving a lot of support to the youngest female team leader. I am very happy that many talented female voices of expectations and challenges have been heard through this opportunity.

How would you like to lead your department in terms of diversity?

Diversity and inclusion were considered the most important when creating the lab.
Currently, the ratio of women is 52%, the appointment of a competent female team leader born in the 1990s, and the appointment of a young leader according to performance. They are now growing into more capable professionals than anyone else. I believe that true change is necessary to foster diversity and inclusion as an organizational culture within a company. For that reason, I want to become a female leader who creates an organizational culture that gives members confidence in developing their capabilities and feels a true sense of belonging through the establishment of an organizational culture with diversity and inclusion. We believe that they are our great resource and the future driving force of innovation.

Please give suggestions on the direction of LG Electronics' diversity and inclusion (nurturing female talent, etc.).

There is a growing recognition of the importance of incorporating diversity and inclusion into corporate culture for a company's long-term business development. As a global company, LG Electronics respects various human differences such as race, ethnicity, age, social class, moral value system, and political beliefs, as well as gender. In particular, LG Electronics has many programs and systems that actively nurture young, core, and female talents.
Through this, we hope to further establish an organizational culture that develops and spreads the synergy of diversity into organizational capabilities by guaranteeing their expertise and autonomy.
