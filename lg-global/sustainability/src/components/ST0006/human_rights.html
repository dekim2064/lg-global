Risk Management
We make sure everyone can work safely and sustainably
at the workplace.
To create a safe and sustainable workplace, we strengthen on-site inspections of production sites, and make sure everyone follows safety standards even higher than the required international rules. To minimize risk to partner companies, we increase the number of people who must self-inspect annually, and we also conduct online training for partners.

We are striving to reduce carbon emissions,
even in the supply chain.
We're building a supply chain greenhouse gas inventory.
We investigate our partner companies' greenhouse gas
emissions, and set up solutions for selected partner companies
that need help. We share our carbon reduction technology
and know-how to educate our partner companies, creating
positive synergy.

Voice Guidance
Voice Guidance audibly lets the user know which button or option you tapped. It also explains functions and current situations. So the user can utilize the product through voice guidance without visual information. The user can use products safely with voice guidance like, "Be careful, hot water is coming out.“

Disability advisory group
Our products reflect diverse opinions to provide a better experience for everyone.

We collaborate closely with a disability advisory group to design our products with everyone in mind. They try out our products and give feedback, so we can make products that benefit everyone. For example, the PuriCare Objet Collection water purifier was created with voice recognition after the group suggested moving the button to the front for easier access. We currently have disability advisory groups in Korea and the US and plan to expand to even more countries soon.

Service environment
improvement
We provide convenient
services to everyone.

LG offers video and audio about product manuals. The video manual uses subtitles and sign language to explain the product's functions and how to use it. We keep expanding our specialized manuals (Video/Audio/Easy Manuals) so everyone can access our products.
LG has offered customer services through sign language consultation and interpreting since 2021. Customers can use sign language to ask for an engineer or how to use products. We continue to increase our support to offer excellent customer service to everyone equally.
