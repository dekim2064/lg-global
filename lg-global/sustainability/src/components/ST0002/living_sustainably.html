Water saving

Smart tech for clean
water and less waste

LG washing machines and dishwashers with
water-saving technology reduce water usage without
sacrificing performance.

Turbo wash

Worried your washing
machine uses too much
water?

LG washing machines with Artificial Intelligence
Direct Drive (AI DD) and TurboWash 360 technology
detect the weight and fabric softness of clothes.
They suggest the optimal wash cycle for each load,
using less water with shorter cycle times.

Our ENERGY STAR-certified washing machines use
25% less energy and 33% less water than
conventional models. ezDispense automatically
dispenses the optimal amount of detergent to
reduce the number of rinses per cycle.

Quad wash

Worried your dishwasher
uses more water than
washing by hand?

Our ultra-efficient dishwashers with ENERGY STAR
certification save an average of 3,870 gallons of
water over their lifecycle through precise control of
water circulation and spray intensity, and only cost
$35 a year to run.

Our dishwashers use QuadWash™ technology with
Multi-Motion spray arms that rotate to separate
food and oil, while TrueSteam™ helps to reduce
water spots, leaving dishware spotless.

Waterless washing

Waterless washing
technology

We recently introduced a revolutionary waterless

consumption and transform the laundry industry. The
new machine utilizes our exclusive Air Wash
technology, using air instead of water to clean
clothes.

Energy saving

Higher energy
efficiency,
lower carbon impact

Our individual efforts combine to make a huge,
positive impact. By choosing energy-efficient
products and reducing our overall energy usage, we
can create a more sustainable future.

DD motor

What makes the DD
(Direct Drive) motor so
energy efficient?

We've perfected Inverter DD motor technology over
four generations, securing global technology
leadership with more than 240 patents on Inverter
DD motors through continuous research and
development. Our innovative motor technology is
already in various appliances, including AC and
refrigerator compressors, reducing power loss by
about 20%.

Our fourth-generation DD motor maximizes energy
efficiency by applying an electric steel plate material.
We've also had a complete production system since
2017, to produce everything from raw materials to
finished products on one seamless production line.
Plus, we've incorporated AI into our Inverter DD
motors for ultimate efficiency.

Knock-on

What's energy efficient
about knocking twice to
see inside the
refrigerator?

Check what's in your refrigerator without opening
the door. Just knock to see inside with Door-in-Door,
cutting cold air loss by 41%, and reducing energy
consumption by 32%.

Door-in-Door technology was developed after a
survey showed that 50% of the time, we open the
refrigerator just to check its contents.

E-waste program

E-waste program for
a sustainable future

LG is committed to collecting e-waste and
batteries to promote circularity. Through our easy,
convenient e-waste program, you can help us make
a positive impact on the environment, inspire
others, and create a better future for all.

E-waste program- Grobal

LG provides collection and processing services for WEEE
(Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) according to
regulations and requirements in each country.
We also offer free collections for large WEEE in Korea,
and we've expanded our service to 78 regions in 47 countries.
We make sure all the WEEE we collect is managed in an
environmentally friendly way.

Small e-waste program- Korea

We're making it easier than ever to recycle small-sized
electronic waste, which has a relatively low recovery rate
compared to large appliances. We're striving to reduce
greenhouse gas and carbon emissions by collecting small
e-waste through nearby LG Best Shops and Resource
Circulation Centers.

Battery recycling program - Korea

We launched a "Battery Return" campaign, encouraging
customers to participate in resource recycling by returning
discarded batteries used in premium LG wireless vacuum
cleaners. We also offer a discount of KRW 10,000 ($8.57)
on new batteries. We've even set up collection boxes for
discarded batteries at Best Shops and other appliance
stores.
