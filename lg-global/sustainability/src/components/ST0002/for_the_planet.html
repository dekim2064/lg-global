Water saving
Smart tech
for clean water
and less waste
LG washing machines and dishwashers with water-saving technology reduce water usage without sacrificing performance.
Turbo wash
Worried your washing machine uses
too much water?
LG washing machines with Artificial Intelligence Direct Drive (AI DD) and TurboWash 360 technology detect the weight and fabric softness of clothes. They suggest the optimal wash cycle for each load, using less water with shorter cycle times.
Our ENERGY STAR-certified washing machines use 25% less energy and 33% less water than conventional models. ezDispense automatically dispenses the optimal amount of detergent to reduce the number of rinses per cycle.
Quad wash
Worried your dishwasher uses more water
than washing by hand?
Our ultra-efficient dishwashers with ENERGY STAR certification save an average o f 3,870 gallons of water over their lifecycle through precise control of water circulation and spray intensity, and only cost $35 a year to run.
Our dishwashers use QuadWash™ technology with Multi-Motion spray arms that rotate to separate food and oil, while TrueSteam™ helps to reduce water spots, leaving dishware spotless.
How much water can ENERGY-STAR certified products save?
We take pride in our outstanding achievements in water conservation and greenhouse gas reduction in North America. Our unwavering commitment to energy efficiency has been recognized with the prestigious "Energy Star Award" on ten occasions between June 2012 and June 2023. Through our Energy Star-certified washing machines and dishwashers, we have successfully reduced greenhouse gas emissions by 3.7 million tons and saved a remarkable 130 billion liters of water. To put this astounding volume in perspective, it is equivalent to filling approximately 53,000 Olympic-sized swimming pools.
Waterless washing
Waterless washing technology
We recently introduced a revolutionary waterless washing machine that aims to reduce water consumption and transform the laundry industry. The new machine utilizes our exclusive Air Wash technology, using air instead of water to clean clothes.
Energy saving
Higher energy
efficiency,
lower carbon
impact
Our individual efforts combine to make a huge, positive impact. By choosing energy-efficient products and reducing our overall energy usage, we can create a more sustainable future.
DD motor
What makes the DD (Direct Drive) motor so
energy efficient?
We've perfected Inverter DD motor technology over four generations, securing global technology leadership with more than 240 patents on Inverter DD motors through continuous research and development. Our innovative motor technology is already in various appliances, including AC and refrigerator compressors, reducing power loss by about 20%.
Our fourth-generation DD motor maximizes energy efficiency by applying an electric steel plate material. We've also had a complete production system since 2017, to produce everything from raw materials to finished products on one seamless production line. Plus, we've incorporated AI into our Inverter DD motors for ultimate efficiency.
Knock-on
What's energy efficient about knocking
twice to see inside the refrigerator?
Check what's in your refrigerator without opening the door. Just knock to see inside with Door-in-Door, cutting cold air loss by 41%, and reducing energy consumption by 32%.
Door-in-Door technology was developed after a survey showed that 50% of the time, we open the refrigerator just to check its contents.
Energy-saving mode
A quieter, more patient
energy-saving mode.
Our upgraded features, including silent energy-saving modes, provide an exceptional customer experience. ThinQ app data helped us identify common customer issues, leading us to develop advanced features, like energy-saving mode, to reduce power usage by 20%.
